Register Teacher

This is a 2-step process:
Step 1 (On this page): Enter teacher name and department, then click Register Teacher.
Step 2 (On device): When asked, the teacher must place their finger on the fingerprint sensor.
Detailed instructions for admin:
1. Confirm the system is in Register Mode (see top of page).
2. Ask the teacher to stand near the ESP32 fingerprint device.
3. Fill in the teacher's Name and Department and click Register Teacher.
4. After you see the success message, tell the teacher: "Now place your finger on the sensor and keep it there until the device confirms."
5. The device will send the fingerprint ID to the backend and complete the registration automatically.
